# High Availability

## What is High Availability?

High Availability (HA) is the ability of a system to remain operational with minimal downtime, even if one server or Availability Zone fails.

## Features

* Reduces downtime
* Improves reliability
* Uses multiple servers
* Supports fault tolerance

## Benefits

* Continuous service
* Better user experience
* Increased reliability
* Business continuity

## Real Life Example

A company deploys its application in two Availability Zones. If one zone fails, the application continues running from the other zone without interrupting users.

## Key Points

* High Availability = Minimum Downtime
* Uses multiple servers or Availability Zones
* Improves reliability
* Supports fault tolerance
