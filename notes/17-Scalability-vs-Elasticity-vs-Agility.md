# Scalability vs Elasticity vs Agility

## Scalability

The ability of a system to handle increasing workloads by adding or upgrading resources.

## Elasticity

The ability of a system to automatically add or remove resources based on workload demand.

## Agility

The ability to quickly develop, deploy, and modify applications or infrastructure to meet changing business needs.

## Difference

| Feature | Scalability | Elasticity | Agility |
|---------|-------------|------------|----------|
| Purpose | Handle increased workload | Automatically adjust resources | Respond quickly to business changes |
| Resource Change | Manual or planned | Automatic | Fast development and deployment |
| Example | Add more EC2 instances | Auto Scaling adds/removes EC2 instances | Launch a new application quickly |

## Key Points

* Scalability = Handle more load
* Elasticity = Automatically scale resources
* Agility = Speed and flexibility
